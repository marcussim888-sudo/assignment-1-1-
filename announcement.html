🌮 Taco 'Bout a Good Time! Food Truck Friday is Here! 🌮


Skip cooking this Friday and join your neighbors for some fantastic local eats! We’ve got three of the city's favorite food trucks rolling in to help us kick off the weekend right. When: This Friday, 5:30 PM - 8:00 PM Where: The Clubhouse Parking Lot What to bring: Your appetite, a picnic blanket, and your neighborhood spirit! Pro-tip: The first 20 residents to stop by the community management table will get a voucher for a free dessert on us! See you there!

🧠 Think You Know It All? Prove It at Neighborhood Trivia Night! 🏆

Grab your smartest neighbors (or the ones who just know an alarming amount of pop culture trivia) and form a team for our Fall Trivia Night! When: Thursday, October 12th at 7:00 PM Where: The Main Resident Lounge Prizes: Gift cards to local coffee shops, ultimate neighborhood bragging rights, and a remarkably shiny plastic trophy! We'll provide light snacks and beverages. Teams can be up to 6 people. Don't have a team? Come anyway, and we’ll match you up with some awesome new friends!

☀️ School's Out, Sun's Out! Join Us for the "Dive Into Summer" Pool Party! 🦩

It’s time to break out the sunscreen and the oversized pool floats! We're celebrating the start of the season with a community splash-bash, and you won't want to miss it. When: Saturday, June 15th from 1:00 PM - 4:00 PM Where: The Main Pool Deck What's Happening: We'll have a live DJ, a DIY hot dog bar, lawn games, and plenty of ice-cold lemonade. Grab your favorite sunglasses and come hang out. Please note: The pool will be open exclusively for residents and up to two guests per apartment during this event.
